MORE ABOUT THE ARTIST

Hello! I am called Space.Signs online, a 15 year old self-taught artist who also loves playing the ukelele and reading books.

I am learning to develop my skills in web and graphic design and fine arts. Most of the content I have produced and posted are requested or commissioned works, like logos and business cards for young entrepreneurs like me from different countries and art communities. It all started with the slime mania in Instagram among my peers. Usually, within the slime community, they buy and sell their products and so I helped them with their branding.

Fun fact: I did all the logos using just my iPhone 6 and the Medibang app. It's also my choice of software on the laptop when I record my speedpainting, this time, using my Wacom Bamboo tablet. In here are just some of my works. For more, you can check my social accounts in Instagram and Youtube.

Though I am still young, my goal is to improve on graphic design and web development while continuing to develop my talent and skills in arts, in particular, illustration and digital arts. I wish to grow and evolve in this field and perhaps work for big animation companies like Pixar or Dreamworks someday.

I hope you like my work and thanks for dropping by!
